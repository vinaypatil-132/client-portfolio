About Dr. Dheeresh KH

I am Dr. Dheeresh KH, a dedicated medical professional with over a decade of experience in internal medicine. My medical journey began at MS Ramaiah Medical College (MSRMC), where I earned my MBBS degree. Driven by a passion for advancing patient care, I pursued an MD in Internal Medicine at the prestigious Dr. Ram Manohar Lohia Hospital (RMLH) in New Delhi. Throughout my career, I have remained committed to staying at the forefront of medical science. I have contributed significantly to the field through numerous case studies, research articles, and publications in both national and international journals. My work reflects a deep understanding of internal medicine and a relentless pursuit of excellence in patient care. I am passionate about making a meaningful impact on the lives of my patients and continue to strive for the highest standards of medical practice.
