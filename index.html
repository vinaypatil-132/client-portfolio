About Dr. Dheeresh KH

Dr. Dheeresh KH, MBBS - MSRMC, MD Internal Medicine - RMLH New Delhi, brings over 10 years of experience in internal medicine. He is a distinguished consultant and a member of the American Association of Physicians. His extensive work includes publishing case studies, research articles, and papers in both national and international medical journals, reflecting his dedication to advancing medical knowledge and patient care.

Services

Chronic Conditions

Expertise in managing chronic diseases like diabetes, hypertension, heart, kidney, and liver diseases, ensuring ongoing care and management.

Infectious Diseases

Skilled in diagnosing and treating infections such as flu, pneumonia, tuberculosis, HIV/AIDS, and more.

Digestive System Disorders

Specializing in conditions affecting the digestive system, including ulcers, GERD, and liver disorders.

Respiratory System

Proficient in treating respiratory conditions like asthma, COPD, and lung cancer.

Endocrine System Disorders

Neurological Disorders
